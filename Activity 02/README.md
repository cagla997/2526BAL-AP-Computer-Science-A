# ACTIVITY 02 - Year-End-Status Determination
 As you know, the students' grade point average, as a decimal number, and the number of subjects they failed in are displayed on their report card at the end of the year. 
 The students' school has the following policy: - If they have a weak grade but their GPA is 50 or above, they pass the class. - If they have a weak grade and their GPA is below 50 but above or equal to 25, the student must take GPA exams. - If they have a weak grade and their GPA is below 25, the student must repeat the year. - If they have no weak grades and their GPA is 50 or above, they pass the class. - If they have no weak grades and their GPA is 70 or above but below 85, the student will be presented with a 
Certificate of Achievement. - If they have no weak grades and their GPA is 85 or above, the student will be presented with a Certificate of High Achievement.
