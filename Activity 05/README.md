# ACTIVITY 05 - School Canteen Daily Sales and Stock Tracking System
 Basic entrepreneurial studies can begin with cooperatives established in schools. In a school without a canteen,
a cooperative formed by students to meet the canteen needs of their friends can be a low-risk and educational entrepreneurial 
activity in a controlled environment. The goal is to track the daily sales of products sold in a school canteen. The program shows how much of each product was sold, the total revenue, and the stock status at the end of the day. This School Canteen Daily Sales and Stock Tracking system first asks the user for 5 product names. After these names are entered, it asks the user for the unit prices of these products. Then it asks for the initial stock quantities.
