# ACTIVITY 03 - Providing Payment Information and Paying Change with the Minimum Number of Coins
As you know, when the bill arrives at the table, you may 
usually pay with a credit card without using cash, but consider a business that only accepts cash.<br>  
Company Payment Policy: <br> - If the bill and the intended tip amount exceed the amount the customer has paid, the missing payment information 
must be provided. <br> - If the bill and the intended tip amount are less than the amount the customer has given, the change information 
must be provided. <br> - If the bill and the intended tip amount are equal to the amount the customer has given, the appreciation information 
must be provided. <br> - Change must be paid with the least amount of change. - The establishment offers 200 TL, 100 TL, 50 TL, 20 TL, 10 TL, 5 TL, and 1 TL bills.
