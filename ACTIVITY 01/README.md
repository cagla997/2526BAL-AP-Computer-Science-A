# ACTIVITY 01 - Body Mass Index Calculation and Current Situation
As you know, our bodies run on energy. This energy is measured in calories. If the calories we consume during the day aren't enough to meet our needs, our bodies will use the remaining energy from our body's reserves (fat) to meet our needs. If we consume 
more than we need during the day, our bodies will store the excess as fat for later use. If we have healthy eating habits and don't have any chronic diseases, our metabolism will function properly, and we 
will lead a HEALTHY life. <br>
To identify any changes in our eating habits that need to be made, we need to calculate our body mass index (bmi) and check our current status.
